# D&D 5e Magic Items & Scrolls by School of Magic

A reference list of magic items and spell scrolls organized by the eight schools
of magic. Spell scrolls are grouped by the school of the spell they contain.
Magic items are grouped by the school whose theme they best represent (warding,
summoning, knowledge, mind-control, raw energy, deception, death, change).

> Sources: D&D 5e System Reference Document (SRD 5.1) and core rulebooks
> (Player's Handbook, Dungeon Master's Guide). Use as a quick-reference for the
> crystal/school mapping in this project.

## Notes on Spell Scrolls

- A spell scroll's rarity scales with spell level:
  - Cantrip — Common
  - 1st–2nd — Common / Uncommon
  - 3rd–4th — Uncommon / Rare
  - 5th–6th — Rare / Very Rare
  - 7th–8th — Very Rare
  - 9th — Legendary
- Some spells appear in multiple schools across editions/source variants
  (e.g., legacy Acid Splash is Conjuration, 2024 version is Evocation).
- The eight schools map 1:1 to the kyber crystals used in this project.
